Pie #3: Mud Pie

[Return to Homepage] <---[Previous Pie: Shepherd's]

Compose pie of soil. Eat it because you want to but not because I told you to

Ingredients:

  • Soil
  • Water
  • Dead leaves
  • Dead grass
  • Optional: Other groundstuffs

Yield:

As much as the bucket can handle

Directions:

  1. Find yourself a bucket or other container capable of dirt
  2. Fill container with soil of choice. I like more clay in mine but it is a matter of personal preference
  3. Add water in accordance to personal taste however you will surely need some to maintain pie integrity
  4. Mix soil and water with hands or implement. Splash in more water until desired consistency
  5. Take desired portion size handfuls of mixture from bucket, shape into pies
  6. Repeat previous step until bucket empty
  7. Leave portioned pies to sundry, or put them in oven(?)
  8. Garnish with leaves and grass, or whatever you think belongs on mud
